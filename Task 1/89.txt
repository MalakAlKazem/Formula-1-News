British team encountered a string of issues that meant Alex Albon and Logan Sargeant were unable to push the car hard. Sargeant encountered a second electronic issue in as many days, the American forced to change his steering wheel, while both cars struggled with overheating engines when running in traffic, which forced them to run them lower and thus cut power. That resulted in Albon ending up 15th and Sargeant 20th – the duo both finishing a lap down.
